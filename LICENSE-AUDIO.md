## What this audio is

The WAV files in this repository are **synthetic voice clones** of public
fictional characters or public figures, generated using the open-source
[fish-speech S2 Pro](https://github.com/fishaudio/fish-speech) model from
publicly-available reference recordings. The reference clips are short
samples of broadcast / streaming material; the synthetic outputs read
short pre-defined phrases (build_failed, tests_passed, etc.) that
VoiceForge plays at runtime as terminal-event feedback.

These clones are **not endorsed by, affiliated with, or licensed from**
the original voice actors, characters, productions, or rights holders.

## Takedown policy

If you are a rights holder (a voice actor, character owner, production
company, or their representative) and you would like a pack removed from
this repository, please open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/humancto/voice-forge-packs/issues)
or email the address listed in the repo's About page.

We will:

1. **Remove the affected pack** within 48 hours of receiving the request.
2. Add the pack name to a public takedown log so contributors don't
   re-submit it.
3. Not require formal DMCA paperwork — a good-faith request from the rights
   holder is enough.

VoiceForge itself (the engine) lives in a [separate repository](https://github.com/humancto/voice-forge)
and is not affected by per-pack takedowns.

## Why this license?

Voice cloning is a dual-use technology. Cloning your own voice or a
friend's voice with consent is fine. Distributing pre-rendered clones of
celebrities or public figures lives in a legal gray area shaped by right-
of-publicity laws (which vary by jurisdiction), the DMCA, and emerging
case law on AI-generated voices.

The license above is calibrated for the realistic risk profile: small,
educational, takedown-friendly distribution by a community of voice-tech
hobbyists. It is not airtight and it is not a substitute for individual
legal judgment about your specific use case. **If your use case is
commercial, public-facing, or involves a person who has not consented,
you should not use these files. Render your own from a voice you have
rights to.**
